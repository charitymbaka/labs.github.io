Swiftlabs is the technical arm of 8TEQ. It ideates and builds all 8teq products.

All products revolve around the fields of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Networks & Telecommunications, and Geospatial Information Systems (GIS).

Swiftlabs is dedicated to ensuring the quality of 8teq products achieve/surpass industry standards.

Data Analytics Labs

This Research lab is the primary Artificial Intelligence research arm. It builds algorithms and data models to enable the functioning of AI applications.

Networks and Telecommunications Lab

This reasearch lab builds systems that enable our clients to share data and communicate securely during the course of their business operations

Geospatial Information Systems (GIS)

The GIS lab develops products that leverage spatial data to better inform real time decision making.

VISCAN FACENET

A Facial Recognition System

VISCAN FaceNet is a facial recognition system that detects faces and matches them to those of persons of interest in a central database.



VISCAN CARNET

A Number Plate Recognition System

ViScan CarNet is a number plate recognition system that detects vehicle number plates and matches them to those of target vehicles in a central database.
